## Lessons Learned
1. Flex column layout (`flex flex-col` + `flex-1` + `shrink-0`) is the correct pattern for dashboard shells with persistent footers — set it up during initial construction
2. "Beta" labels are product decisions — when they're removed, grep the entire codebase including build artifacts to ensure zero residue
3. /rrr after every feature, not at session end — prevents reaper accumulation
